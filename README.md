Crypto Breaks the Rules You Didn’t Know Existed
Risk management in crypto is different from traditional investing. Diversification still matters, but it extends beyond assets to include networks, wallets, and interaction patterns. Spreading activity across different platforms and avoiding overexposure to a single protocol can reduce potential losses.
Market psychology plays a significant role in crypto. Fear and greed often drive decisions more than fundamentals. Understanding these emotional cycles can provide an advantage, but it requires discipline and awareness. Acting based on impulse rather than strategy is one of the most common mistakes.
